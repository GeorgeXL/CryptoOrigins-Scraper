# Topic Hierarchy

This is the intended topic/storyline hierarchy for the editorial agent.

Keep this taxonomy slim. Topics are predetermined buckets, not free-form labels. Each summary should map to the most specific existing subcategory below. If nothing fits, the agent should leave the topic unset or propose a new subcategory for human approval.

Ignore old topic values such as `adoption`, `economic`, `historical`, `technology`, `institutional`, `price`, `market`, or `regulation`. They are legacy noise, not the taxonomy.

Tags are separate. Tags name concrete entities or terms grounded in the summary/source, such as people, companies, protocols, places, products, or named events.

## Bitcoin

Bitcoin-native history, protocol, network, mining, wallets, scaling, and adoption.

- Early Bitcoin history
- Satoshi identity
- Protocol development
- Bitcoin Core
- BIPs and upgrades
- Soft forks and hard forks
- Mining evolution
- Mining pools
- Mining companies
- Halving events
- Wallet development
- Scaling and Layer 2
- Node infrastructure
- Privacy and self-custody
- Bitcoin adoption

## Markets

Price, cycles, liquidity, trading, and investment products.

- Bitcoin price action
- Market cycles
- Liquidity and flows
- Institutional inflows
- Trading activity
- Derivatives
- ETFs and investment products
- Safe haven narrative

## Macro & Policy

Macroeconomic context, government action, regulation, legal disputes, tax, and public policy.

- Inflation
- Interest rates

- Central banks
- Banking stress

- Labor market
- Housing
- Bailouts and stimulus
- Global growth and recession
- Bitcoin regulation
- Securities regulation
- Banking regulation
- Tax policy
- Sanctions and state actions
- Legal cases
- Government adoption
- CBDCs

## Companies & Adoption

Companies, infrastructure, payments, custody, business adoption, and failures involving service providers.

- Exchanges

- Startup funding
- Enterprise blockchain adoption
- Data and analytics

- Law enforcement
- Operational security

## Other Crypto

Non-Bitcoin crypto only when it is historically relevant enough to include.

- Altcoin ecosystems
- Layer 1 networks
- Ethereum and smart contracts
- Stablecoins
- DeFi
- NFTs and gaming
- Cross-chain infrastructure
